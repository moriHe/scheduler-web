1. Access Data and Hosting

When visiting this website, information is automatically processed by the hosting provider DigitalOcean, LLC and stored in so-called server log files. This information includes:

  • Visited page
  • Date and time of access
  • Amount of data transferred
  • Referrer URL
  • Browser used
  • Operating system used
  • IP address (anonymized or truncated where possible)

Processing is carried out in accordance with Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR based on the legitimate interest in the stable and secure provision of this website.

The servers are located in data centers operated by DigitalOcean, LLC, 101 Avenue of the Americas, 10th Floor, New York, NY 10013, USA. A data processing agreement (DPA) exists with DigitalOcean. Data processing may take place outside the EU. DigitalOcean provides appropriate safeguards in accordance with Art. 46 GDPR.

2. Use of Web Technologies

This website uses Tailwind CSS and Ionicons, which are loaded via a content delivery network (CDN). Due to technical reasons, requests may be made to third-party servers (e.g. jsdelivr or unpkg).

IP addresses may also be transmitted in the process. However, we do not store or evaluate them.

3. No Cookies, No Tracking

This website uses no cookies, no web tracking, and does not actively collect any personal data (e.g. via forms).

4. Use of LocalStorage

This website uses LocalStorage to store the names you add locally on your device. This storage is solely intended to enhance your user experience, so that you don’t have to re-enter names during each session. This data is never shared with third parties.

You can delete this data at any time via your browser settings. LocalStorage is not used for tracking or advertising and is strictly limited to the functionality of the website.

5. Metrics

We use anonymous metrics to understand how users interact with our website and where traffic originates. This information helps us improve the website and provide a better experience for visitors. We do not collect your personal data, serve personalized ads, or sell your data to third parties.

Our website is monitored using Simple Analytics, a privacy-friendly web analytics provider. For more information, visit https://www.simpleanalytics.com/.

6. Your Rights

You have the right at any time to access your stored personal data, and to request correction, deletion, or restriction of processing. You also have the right to file a complaint with a competent data protection authority.
